Winning the AI Race Part 5: President Trump on the AI Action Plan

Trade Achievements and International Relations

• Announced a major trade deal with Japan worth $550 billion, reducing tariffs from 25% to 15% while Japan opens its markets to American businesses
• Completed deals with Philippines, Indonesia, and ongoing negotiations with China, UK, and European Union
• Secured agreement from NATO allies to increase defense spending from 2% to 5% of GDP, with Europe paying 100% of military equipment costs for Ukraine

AI Leadership and Competition Strategy

• Declared America's intention to win the global AI race, emphasizing the need to prevent foreign nations from controlling AI algorithms
• Positioned AI as the most important technological revolution in history, comparing its significance to the space age
• Stressed that America currently leads China "very substantially" in AI development and must maintain this advantage

Regulatory Framework and Federal Standards

• Called for elimination of complex intellectual property rules that require payment for every piece of content AI systems learn from, arguing this approach is unworkable
• Demanded a single federal standard for AI regulation instead of 50 different state regulations to prevent restrictive states from hampering development
• Announced executive order requiring elimination of 10 old regulations for every new regulation implemented

Infrastructure and Energy Policy

• Unveiled plan for massive AI infrastructure investment including new data centers, semiconductor facilities, and power plants
• Granted companies the right to build their own power plants and sell excess energy back to the grid
• Terminated California's car emission rules and the "Green New Deal" policies, promoting "drill baby drill" and nuclear energy expansion

Investment Commitments and Economic Impact

• Announced nearly $17 trillion in new investments coming to America, with Meta, Amazon, Google, and Microsoft investing $320+ billion in data centers and AI infrastructure
• VIDIA committed $500 billion over four years for AI development
• Emphasized these investments will create thousands of high-paying jobs including blue-collar positions

Anti-Woke AI Policies

• Terminated Biden's diversity, equity, and inclusion requirements for AI development
• Signed executive order banning federal procurement of AI technology with partisan bias or ideological agendas
• Mandated that government AI systems pursue "truth, fairness, and strict impartiality" rather than woke ideology

Export Strategy and Global Dominance

• Repealed the "Biden diffusion rule" that restricted AI exports, arguing it drove allies toward China
• Signed executive order to make America an "AI export powerhouse" through expanded chip, software, and data storage exports
• Emphasized goal of getting the entire world running on American AI technology backbone

Key Executive Orders Signed

• Fast-track federal permitting for AI infrastructure projects including data centers and power plants
• Promotion of American AI model exports to ensure global dominance
• Ban on federal procurement of ideologically biased AI systems, requiring neutrality and accuracy
